About the Researcher

01 — Personal Profile

Gregorius Peitra, 2025

Background

Gregorius Peitra Dirgantara Sakti Nikula Asmara is an early-career researcher focusing on digital transformation, customer experience, artificial intelligence, and consumer behavior.

His research examines how technology, service innovation, and sustainability values influence customer trust, satisfaction, loyalty, engagement, and behavioral intention. His research journey combines academic research with industry experience through customer experience analytics, digital business, and research consulting.

His work sits at the intersection of digital innovation and sustainable business models, with a focus on emerging markets where digital transformation is rapidly reshaping consumer expectations and business models.

Research Positioning

"Exploring how digital transformation, sustainability, and consumer behavior shape future customer experiences in emerging markets."

This positioning guides a coherent research agenda spanning digital CX, AI-driven service innovation, and sustainable consumption patterns — united by a focus on the customer as the unit of analysis.
