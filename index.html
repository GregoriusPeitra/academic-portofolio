About

Research Identity

Gregorius Peitra is an early-career researcher focusing on digital transformation, customer experience, artificial intelligence, and consumer behavior.

His research examines how technology, service innovation, and sustainability values influence customer trust, satisfaction, loyalty, engagement, and behavioral intention. His journey combines academic research with industry experience through customer experience analytics, digital business, and research consulting.

"My long-term research agenda focuses on developing an integrated understanding of customer experience transformation in digital and sustainable ecosystems."
